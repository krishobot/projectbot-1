# astack + tbrain

**A virtual company you run from one terminal.**
Thirteen specialised AI teammates. One brain that doesn't forget. One human at the keyboard.

**Live app:** https://projectbot-1.vercel.app — sign in to use.

---

## Why this exists

Solo founders don't lose to bad ideas. They lose to context evaporating between tabs, scrollback, and a Notion they stopped opening in week three.

**astack** gives you a permanent staff of 13 — Executive, Engineering, Design, QA, Release, DevEx, Security, Product, Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, Ops, Data — each one a Claude Code session pre-scoped to its charter and its skills.

**tbrain** is the staff memory. Markdown source-of-truth + Postgres + pgvector. Every person, deal, decision, meeting, and shipped artifact gets a page. Every team reads from the same brain. Nothing important lives in chat history.

One human. Thirteen roles. Zero meetings.

---

## How to use it

Two paths.

**1. The hosted app — free.** Visit [projectbot-1.vercel.app](https://projectbot-1.vercel.app), sign in with email, launch any team from the browser. The brain runs locally on your machine via the desktop companion — your data never leaves your laptop.

**2. The full source — included with any [pack](https://projectbot-1.vercel.app/packs).** Buy a pack on Gumroad and you get collaborator access to this private repo. Clone, fork, customise. Lifetime updates via `git pull`. See [MANUAL.md](MANUAL.md) for the install path.

## The four rules every team follows

1. **Markdown is source of truth** — not chat, not tickets.
2. **Single writer per file** — one team owns each page; others read.
3. **Append-only evidence trails** — editable summary on top, immutable timeline below.
4. **No fabricated facts** — every claim links to a real source.
